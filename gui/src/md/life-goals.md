# Life Goals Process

Your goal here is to find a set of extremely simple and natural (for you) filters against which you can pass through your different *goals ideas*.

For this, you first need to know your values and if you live your life in alignment with them today.
After, your filters are basically the response to *Does this pass through my values?* question.

To do this, `LMMs` are actually pretty good tools if given a prompt that won't take suck your dick validating your bullshit each message.

## The Process

### 1. The Context

Start by responding to these questions so the `LLM` can have the needed context about your life and personality and can adapt its responses to you.
Be as factual and detailed as you can. Tell your life story if you need too :

- Age, where you live, relationship status
- What you do for money right now
- What your life looks like on paper vs how it actually feels
- What changed in the last 1-2 years that made you start questioning things

### 2. The Pre-flight Questions

Then anwser these pre-flight questions to kickstart the `LLM` in the good direction.
Same here, be factual, detailed and as honest as you can (and take your time) : 

1. What have you actually built or done in your life that you're proud of — not feelings, concrete things
2. What's the worst failure you've had in the last 3 years and what did you conclude from it
3. What makes you viscerally angry when you see it in the world
4. When did you last feel genuinely alive — not happy, alive
5. What project do you keep coming back to in your head that you've never fully committed to and why

### 3. Actually speak with the `LLM`

Now, just hit the button `Get Prompt` and past it in the one of your choice.
Have a conversation with it until you land with certainty on your values and your filters.

### 4. Your final goals

Pass your goals through the filters to : 

 - discard them if they conflict
 - prioritize them

You should have 3-4 life goals tops.
It could be huge things that gonna take a decade each to realize.
